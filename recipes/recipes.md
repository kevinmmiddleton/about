# Broccoli Potato Cheddar Soup

---
tags: vegetarian, main, quick
servings: 4
photo: https://middleton.io/recipes/images/potato-brocolli-cheddar-soup.jpg
---

Comfort food that comes together fast in the Instant Pot. Partially blend at the end if you want it creamier.

## Ingredients

- 60g butter
- 6g garlic, minced
- 600g chicken broth (or water + 25g Better Than Bouillon)
- 300g potatoes, peeled and diced into 1cm cubes
- 300g broccoli florets, chopped small (include tender inner stems)
- 3g onion powder
- 3g salt
- 2g black pepper
- 1g MSG
- 0.5g cayenne
- 400g whole milk
- 20g flour
- 50g cold milk (for slurry)
- 225g sharp cheddar, shredded

## Instructions

1. Set Instant Pot to sauté mode. Melt butter, cook garlic 1 minute.
2. Add broth, potatoes, broccoli, onion powder, salt, pepper, MSG, and cayenne. Stir.
3. Pressure cook on high for 5 minutes.
4. Quick release.
5. Switch to sauté mode. Whisk 20g flour with 50g cold milk into a smooth slurry. Stir into soup, simmer 2-3 minutes until thickened.
6. Stir in 400g whole milk.
7. Add cheddar a handful at a time, stirring until melted.
8. Taste and adjust seasoning.
9. Optionally blend partially for a creamier texture.

# Sausage and Cheese Quiche

---
tags: eggs, pork, breakfast, main, weekend-project
servings: 6-8 slices
photo: https://middleton.io/recipes/images/sausag-cheese-quiche.jpg
---

## Ingredients

### For the Crust
- 1 pie crust, blind baked

### For the Custard
- 200g eggs
- 200g milk
- 30g butter, melted
- 3g garlic powder
- 3g onion powder
- Black pepper to taste
- Red pepper flakes to taste
- Sage to taste
- Parsley to taste

### For the Filling
- 150-200g sausage
- 100-120g cheese, shredded

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 175°C / 350°F.
2. Brown the sausage in a skillet, breaking into crumbles. Drain excess fat.
3. Whisk together eggs, milk, melted butter, and all spices.
4. Layer sausage and cheese into the blind-baked crust.
5. Pour custard over the filling.
6. Bake 35-45 minutes until edges are set and center has a slight jiggle.
7. Rest 10-15 minutes before slicing.

# Chick-fil-A Style Chicken

---
tags: chicken, main, weekend-project
servings: 2-3
source: https://jasonfarmer.com/chick-fil-a-chicken-sandwich/
photo: https://middleton.io/recipes/images/chicken.jpg
---

Modified version with no sugar and extra cayenne.

## Ingredients

### For the Chicken
- 397g boneless skinless tenders, thighs, or breasts

### For the Brine
- 1000ml water
- 67g Morton's coarse kosher salt
- 12g MSG
- 6g paprika
- 0.5g black pepper, coarse
- 5g mustard powder
- 1g cayenne

### For the Milk Wash
- 236ml skim milk
- 2 eggs

### For the Seasoned Coater
- 120g all-purpose flour
- 4.5g Morton's coarse kosher salt
- 4g MSG
- 11g nonfat milk powder
- 3.5g baking powder
- 0.5g white pepper
- 5g mustard powder
- 1g cayenne

## Instructions

1. Mix brine dry ingredients into room temperature water until dissolved. Brine tenders 2-4 hours (breasts 6-8 hours).
2. Whisk milk and eggs together for the milk wash.
3. Mix and sift all coater ingredients. Drizzle in 2 tbsp milk wash and crumble through with your hands before breading.
4. Heat peanut oil to 163°C / 325°F.
5. Pull chicken from brine, pat dry.
6. Dip in milk wash, shake off excess.
7. Press into seasoned coater.
8. Fry about 4 minutes (adjust for thickness).
9. Rest on wire rack a couple minutes before serving.

# Honey-Teriyaki Chicken Thighs

---
tags: chicken, main, asian, quick
servings: 3-4
photo: https://middleton.io/recipes/images/teriyaki-chicken.jpg
---

## Ingredients

- 680g boneless skinless chicken thighs
- 90g soy sauce
- 90g dry white wine
- 45g honey
- 10g garlic, minced
- 2g red pepper flakes
- 10g neutral oil

## Instructions

1. Whisk soy sauce, wine, honey, garlic, and pepper flakes.
2. Marinate chicken 30-60 minutes.
3. Heat pan with oil over medium-high.
4. Sear thighs 4-5 minutes per side.
5. Add remaining marinade and simmer until thick and glossy.
6. Reduce until sauce coats the chicken.

# Crispy Pork Schnitzel

---
tags: pork, main, quick
servings: 2
photo: https://middleton.io/recipes/images/schnitzel.jpg
---

## Ingredients

- 450g pork cutlets, pounded thin
- 60g all-purpose flour
- 2 large eggs (about 100g)
- 90g panko breadcrumbs
- 5g salt
- 2g black pepper
- 2g onion powder
- About 500ml neutral oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Season pork with salt, pepper, and onion powder.
2. Set up dredge station: flour, beaten eggs, panko.
3. Coat pork fully, pressing crumbs in.
4. Heat oil to 175°C / 350°F.
5. Fry 2-3 minutes per side until golden.
6. Drain on wire rack and rest 2 minutes before serving.

# Slow-Cooked Creamy Scrambled Eggs

---
tags: eggs, breakfast, quick
servings: 2
photo: https://middleton.io/recipes/images/eggs.jpg
---

Low and slow is the secret. Remove from heat just before they're done.

## Ingredients

- 6 large eggs (about 300g)
- 30g unsalted butter
- 30ml whole milk
- 3g salt

## Instructions

1. Whisk eggs, milk, and salt.
2. Melt butter in a nonstick pan over very low heat.
3. Add eggs and stir constantly with a silicone spatula.
4. Cook slowly 8-10 minutes until soft and custardy.
5. Remove just before fully set; residual heat finishes them.

# Costco-Style Blueberry Muffins

---
tags: vegetarian, breakfast, snack, weekend-project
servings: 8 large muffins
photo: https://middleton.io/recipes/images/blueberry-muffins.jpg
---

The two-temperature bake gives you those big domed tops.

## Ingredients

- 300g all-purpose flour (plus 10g for tossing berries)
- 150g granulated sugar
- 10g baking powder
- 4g salt
- 140g unsalted butter, melted
- 2 large eggs (about 100g)
- 180ml whole milk
- 8g vanilla extract
- 220g fresh blueberries

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 205°C / 400°F. Line or grease a muffin tin.
2. Whisk 300g flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
3. Whisk melted butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla in a separate bowl.
4. Fold wet into dry until just combined.
5. Toss blueberries with 10g flour, then gently fold into batter.
6. Fill cups almost to the top.
7. Bake 5 minutes at 205°C, then reduce to 175°C / 350°F and bake 18-22 minutes more.
8. Cool 10 minutes before removing.
