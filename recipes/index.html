+ Why does this exist?

I try to keep it easy in the kitchen. Instead of measuring cups, I use a food scale for everything. One of my biggest pet peeves when reading recipes: I don't want to scroll past someone's childhood memories to find the ingredient list and recipe.

So I'm sharing some of my actual recipes without the stories. Some of these are original, some borrowed, adapted, adjusted. Enjoy them!
