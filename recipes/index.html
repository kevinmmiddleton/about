Kevin's Recipe Book

A small collection of things I actually cook.

‚ú¶ Why does this exist?

I try to keep it easy in the kitchen. I use a food scale for everything. One of my biggest pet peeves: I don't want to scroll past someone's childhood memories to find the ingredient list and recipe. I tweak recipes until they work for me, then forget what I changed.

This is the fix: my actual recipes, in grams, with no preamble. Some of these are original, some borrowed, adapted, adjusted, and written down so I stop texting myself.

Made using a food scale by a picky eater.
