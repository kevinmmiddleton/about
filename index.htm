About Me

In my experience, teams usually know what's wrong. They just need someone willing to name it and make it safe to act on. That's usually where I start.

What I Mean by "Full Stack PM"

I work across people, process, and product. I'm hands-on at every level: running meetings, cleaning up details, digging into data, or building a quick prototype. I turn messy problems into clear systems teams can own. I build with AI where it actually helps. Most recently that's meant AI-powered compliance tools, LLM copilots, and growth experiments that drove 50x conversion lift.

Most of my best work happens between teams that don't usually talk to each other. Engineering and support. Product and marketing. I broker the agreements, bring the context, and make sure nobody's working in the dark.

Outside of work, I enjoy cooking, spending time with my cats, and getting lost in pop culture and trivia.
