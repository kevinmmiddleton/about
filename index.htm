About Me

I love working with collaborative teams and learning from curious, thoughtful people. I care about how teams communicate, how decisions get made, and how to keep work moving without unnecessary friction.

I work across people, process, and product. I’m hands-on at every level: running meetings, cleaning up details, digging into data, or building a quick prototype. I turn ambiguity into maintainable systems teams can own, and I use AI where it removes friction or speeds things up.
