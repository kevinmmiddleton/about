Co-branded Partner Sites

 

Expanding Legal Access, Enabling Partners, and Growing Brand Awareness

 

Presented by Kevin Middleton

Introduction

 

Problem Statement: Addressing the challenge of scaling and customizing legal services for partners.

 

My Role: Leading the initiative to develop co-branded partner sites.

Mission Alignment

Rocket Lawyer's Vision: Everyone deserves affordable and simple legal services.

 

Project's Contribution: Expanding Rocket Lawyer's reach in Europe and establishing a new revenue channel.

Understanding the Challenge

User Focus: Tailoring solutions for partners and their customers.

 

Pain Points Addressed: Partner authentication, co-branding, dynamic pricing, partner-specific content.

 

Additional Challenges:

  • Rapid project ramp-up, with first demo leading to contract signing within months.
  • Time zone coordination across San Francisco, UK (+8), and France (+9).
  • Working around entire partner staff vacation period in August.
  • Last-minute Angular upgrade to improve site performance for slower connections.

The Solution

Features: Partner authentication (OpenID Connect + Custom Connector), co-branding, dynamic pricing.

Collaboration and Execution

Cross-Functional Teams: Collaboration with UX, marketing, engineering, content, and legal teams.

 

Scope Management: Balancing rapid delivery with quality and scalability.

 

Resource Negotiation: Securing additional resources for ecommerce, reporting, and front-end development.

Reflection and Key Decisions

Critical Decisions: Descoping advanced reporting for scalability.

 

Manager's Quote: Feedback from Rob Elhardt, VP of Product Management.

Lessons Learned

Collaboration and Leadership: My early days and late nights led to the project's success. A close, trusting relationship with my tech lead ensured we stayed on the same page.

 

Advocacy and Protection: Advocating for additional resources and protecting my team from extra work were crucial for staying focused and on schedule.

 

Building for Scale: Focusing on scalability, not just for a single customer, but for future partner integrations.
