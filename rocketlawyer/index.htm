Rocket Lawyer

Co-branded Partner Sites

 

Expanding Legal Access, Enabling Partners, and Growing Brand Awareness

 

Presented by Kevin Middleton

Introduction

 

Problem Statement: Addressing the challenge of scaling and customizing legal services for partners.

 

My Role: Leading the initiative to develop co-branded partner sites.

Mission Alignment

Rocket Lawyer's Vision: Everyone deserves affordable and simple legal services.

 

Project's Contribution: Expanding Rocket Lawyer's reach in Europe and establishing a new revenue channel.

Understanding the Challenge

 

User Focus: Tailoring solutions for Covéa’s 3 brands with 11.5 million total members (and future partners) – integrating legal solutions into their insurance products.

 

Pain Points Addressed: Providing partner customers with digital legal documents, eSigning, and 'Ask A Lawyer' capabilities.

The Solution

 

Summary

Developed a comprehensive, tailored solution to integrate Rocket Lawyer's legal services with partners, focusing on seamless authentication, co-branding, customized pricing, and exclusive content to expand legal access and market reach.

 

Features

  • Partner authentication
    • OpenID Connect + Custom Connector
  • Co-branding
  • Partner-specific pricing (free & discounted)
  • Partner-exclusive content

Collaboration and Execution

Cross-Functional Teams: Collaboration with engineering, design, marketing, and legal content teams.

 

Scope Management: Balancing rapid delivery with quality and scalability.

 

Resource Negotiation: Securing additional resources for ecommerce, reporting, and front-end development.

Reflection and Key Decisions

 

On-Time Delivery: Ensuring the project met crucial deadlines, including the first partner demo in June, leading to a successful contract signing.

 

Prioritization: Delivering a slim but impactful Minimum Lovable Product (MLP), prioritizing features that provided the most value to partners.

 

Protecting the Team: Taking on non-essential tasks like documentation, meetings, and diagram creation from engineers, allowing them to focus on development.

 

Building for Scale: Designed solutions with future scalability in mind, ensuring they could be adapted for additional partners and markets.

 

Descoping as Needed: Pulled advanced reporting (didn't scale).

Impact and Results

Traffic Increase:

Business Outcomes:

Challenges

  • Rapid project ramp-up: hired in late April, with first partner demo in June leading to contract signing.

     

  • Time zone coordination across San Francisco, UK (+8 hours), and France (+9 hours).

     

  • Partner Staff Vacation: Navigating Covéa’s August vacation period.

     
  • Angular Upgrade: Necessary to improve site performance.

Lessons Learned

 

Collaboration and Leadership: My early days and late nights led to the project's success. A close, trusting relationship with my tech lead ensured we stayed on the same page.

 

Advocacy and Protection: Advocating for additional resources and protecting my team from extra work were crucial for staying focused and on schedule.

 

Building for Scale: Focusing on scalability, not just for a single customer, but for future partner integrations.
