The Challenge

 

User Focus

Tailoring solutions for Covéa’s 3 brands with 11.5 million total members (and future partners) – integrating legal solutions into their insurance products.

 

Pain Points Addressed

Providing partner customers with digital legal documents, eSigning, and 'Ask A Lawyer' capabilities.

Solution

Overview

Developed a comprehensive, tailored solution to integrate Rocket Lawyer's legal services with partners, focusing on seamless authentication, co-branding, customized pricing, and exclusive content to expand legal access and market reach.

 

Features

  • Partner Authentication: Implemented using OpenID Connect for robust, scalable partner integration.
  • Custom Connector: Facilitated partner-specific integrations.
  • Partner Config API: Enabled customizable settings and frontend brandability, aligning with partner-specific requirements.
  • Reporting Capabilities: Enhanced existing events structure to provide detailed, partner-specific reporting.
  • Co-Branding and Partner-Specific Pricing: Customizable for each partner.
  • Partner-Exclusive Content: Tailored legal solutions and resources.

Technical Challenges

 

Authentication and Connector

 

Challenge: Required a robust, scalable solution for partner authentication within OpenID Connect constraints.

 

Solution: Developed a custom Connector in addition to OpenID Connect, ensuring scalability and flexibility.

Technical Challenges

 

Partner Config API

 

Challenge: Needed a system for flexible, multi-brand organization settings and associations.

 

Solution: Created the Partner Config API for customizable partner settings, frontend brandability, and differentiating Rocket Lawyer's country websites as unique brands.

Technical Challenges

 

Reporting Capabilities

 

Challenge: Essential to track and report partner usage for usage-based billing.

 

Solution: Enhanced the in-house user events framework, enabling detailed usage reporting and billing model support.
