✨ How we'd test these

1 · Floating mobile CTA

Hypothesis: a persistent primary+secondary CTA converts intent that forms mid-scroll (the page runs ~15 screens on mobile with no CTA in view).

Validate: A/B vs current; guardrail that hero-CTA clicks don't just shift, and that signups still activate.

Metric: mobile new-customer CVR; secondary: bar CTR, checkout starts.

Note: a signup lift that doesn't activate is a red flag, not a win.

2 · Affordability section

Hypothesis: transparent pricing with per-day framing on the homepage removes price uncertainty for cost-anxious visitors before they click away.

Validate: A/B section on/off; MVT the framing ($/day vs $/mo vs savings-led).

Metric: homepage→plans CTR and new-customer CVR; watch 30-day activation as the quality guardrail.

3 · Surfaced plan-match quiz

Hypothesis: presenting the buried goal-picker as an obviously interactive module lifts quiz starts and sends better-qualified traffic to plans.

Validate: A/B module vs current text links.

Metric: quiz starts and completions; downstream plan-page CVR.
